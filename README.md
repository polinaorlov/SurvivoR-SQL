
Survivor Dashboard

The main page explores whether voting with the majority is linked to getting far in the game. 
“Right-Side Tribals (%)” is the share of Tribal Councils (TC) where a castaway voted for 
the person who was ultimately voted out. Castaways who attended less than 3 TCs are omitted 
to avoid skewing the results. Finish Place is final position of a Castaway (e.g., 1=winner). 
Use the trend and group summaries to compare winners, finalists, pre- and post- jury boots.

Second drill-through page shows individual castaway voting history, available by clicking on the 
scatter plot points from page 1.
